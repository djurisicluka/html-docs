Shopping + perfume reality check

Streetwear / contemporary

  • Represent London: flagship on Wardour Street, but likely closed by your ETA. Skip tonight.
  • END. London: Broadwick Street, usually only realistic if you rush before 8 PM.
  • Machine-A: strong contemporary designer/streetwear, but also only realistic before 8 PM.
  • Selfridges: best late option because it stays open later and has broad fashion/fragrance coverage.

Niche fragrance

  • Argos: no confirmed central-London stop worth chasing tonight from quick research.
  • Bloom Perfumery / Les Senteurs / Le Labo Soho: good niche options, but likely closed by the time you arrive.
  • Selfridges Fragrance Hall: safest late perfume stop tonight.
  • Liberty / Perfumer H: only worth it if you are nearby before closing.

If shopping becomes the priority

At Buckingham Palace, take 5 minutes for photos, then go straight to Soho/Carnaby. You might catch the final minutes of END., Machine-A, or Liberty before 8 PM. After 8 PM, stop chasing closed boutiques and go to Selfridges.
