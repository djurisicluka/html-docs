One-day Zurich itinerary

Designed to be scenic but easy after the flight, with no complicated detours.

🧳
1:50–3:15 PM

Land, reach the hotel, drop bags

Take a taxi/Uber to the hotel because of the heavy luggage. Check in, freshen up and leave the bags behind before walking.

🏛️
3:30–5:15 PM

Bahnhofstrasse, Augustinergasse and Lindenhof

Start with central Zurich’s polished shopping street, then move into the prettier old lanes and the Lindenhof viewpoint over the Limmat.

Lindenhof map
⛪
5:15–6:30 PM

Old Town, river and iconic churches

Walk through Münsterhof, Fraumünster and across the river toward Grossmünster and Limmatquai.

🌊
6:30–7:30 PM

Lake Zurich promenade

Continue to Bürkliplatz or Bellevue for an easy golden-hour lake walk and photos.

Bürkliplatz map
🍽️
7:30–9:00 PM

Swiss dinner

Good simple options: Zeughauskeller for traditional Swiss dishes, Zunfthaus zur Waag for something more polished, or Sternen Grill for casual food.

🌙
9:00 PM onward

Short Niederdorf walk, then sleep

Enjoy a final old-town stroll but keep the night easy. Both departures are around noon the next day.
